Types Of Student On Studying

  1. Those who study every subject on every single day.
  2. Those who study, when it’s required.
  3. Those who study when it’s actually required.

Master Gp Said: The students going with second approach are the best ones, as they are not too early or too late. The ideal time for starting preparation if you haven’t studied during sessional exams is one month. If you’ve studied just appropriate before then it is 14 days.

Three types of students on doing assignments

  1. Those who do their assignments before time.
  2. Those who do their assignments on time.
  3. Those who never do their assignments or when submission is closed.

Master Gp Said: : Blessed are those who do their assignments a little early before actual submission time. Do it on your own if you think it will prepare you in any way in your future or just copy it. Help your friends by providing them completed assignments this will create a state of mutual symbiosis and thus helping you too.

Three types of students on relationship with teacher

  1. Puppets of teacher’s hand.
  2. Those who mingles.
  3. Lone wolves.

Master Gp Said: It is seen that students who lick their teacher’s toes (Rubbing board, always sitting in first three rows, helping teachers without being asked for help) might succeed early or with little efforts but it eventually weakens their qualities of self-reliance and confidence.

The type two persona is best as you are doing what is just needed. These students follow the commands of their teachers. Never disappoint them, never argues.

Worst are the lone wolves, who for their self-pride or esteem argue with them and got less attention thereafter. The relationship ends with a pitiful state of securing less than 50% internal marks. It can be avoided by following the second point.

Three types of subjects

  1. Purely theoretical.
  2. Numerical and logic based.
  3. Non-sense.

Master Gp Said: It depends person to person, some students are good at understanding and retaining theories while others are competent in solving logic based or numerical problems. Find your type in first semester and remember that what remains beyond your type is what you have to struggle with.

Generally, engineering is easy for students who are expert of type 2, but theoretical subjects too have long been known as hurdles.

Type 3 is comical, they are easy most of the times, not helpful in future in any way. So, avoid in-depth preparation of these subjects and focus on previous years papers..

Three type of Student mindset

  1. Always fearful.
  2. Calm and focused.
  3. Always fearless.

Master Gp Said: Fearful are those who prepare one night before exam, Calm and focused are those who studied appropriately, Fearless are those who have nothing to study (that they’ve revised thrice). The second approach is rewarding in academic as well future career.

Three types of paper writing approach

  1. 40 pages filled in good handwriting with two supplementary sheets attached.
  2. More than 25 pages filled in good handwriting.
  3. Less than 20 pages filled in bad handwriting.

Master Gp Said: Approach 2 is best as 40 sheets are actually more than enough, it is seen that students who write letters of bumble bee size can take supplementary. More than 25 pages are just appropriate if they are well written (covered in next case). But never try the third approach.

After consulting Master Gp, we are presenting you ten semester exam skills.

  1. Count number of pages it should not less or more else your sheet will not be checked.
  2. Do not use Pens other than Blue and Black.
  3. Make margins it will reduce the size of writing area hence you can fill more sheets.
  4. Try to make diagrams of as many questions as you can, a good impact will be set on the examiners mind.
  5. Always start the longest section first, it will help you to secure more points, attempt one or two number questions in the end.
  6. Try to attempt each and every question, no matter what, chances of a good examiner is 50%.
  7. Tell your buddies to cheat only after you’ve done, else you’ll miss your own paper.
  8. Mark last pages with the heading ‘ROUGH’.
  9. After attempting paper do not discuss the question paper, as the gone will never be back again. Just wait for the result to be announced.
  10. When friends ask about How much have you been prepared, instead of saying “Nothing man, I’ll get back” or “I’ve studied everything, I’ll surely bang the paper” Just tell them this – “Enough to avoid back”.
